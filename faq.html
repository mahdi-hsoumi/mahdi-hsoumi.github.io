Do you work with people in (blank) industry?

We work with every industry. Industry-specific knowledge is not the most important piece of marketing. You are an expert in your industry, and we are experts in digital marketing. We team up with you to use your industry knowledge along with our marketing expertise to reach your exact audience

Would my business benefit from digital marketing services?

Every single brand has to have an online representation of the business. However, your digital footprint won't have any impact if you don't market it successfully. Every business would benefit from an ongoing, comprehensive digital marketing strategy and the support of an expert marketing team.

What is the first step to engage with Meradise?

Whenever someone reaches out to us, the first step is to have a Fact-Finding Meeting. The goal of this meeting is for you to tell us about your business, and for us to listen. During this meeting, we don’t try to sell anything. We are there simply to learn. Afterwards, if we are a good fit for each other, we will schedule a follow-up meeting to discuss services that may be effective for your business.

How are you different from others?

Our vision and focus is in-line with yours, We offer only tailored solutions because your business has unique challenges, goals, and needs. We spend a lot of time one-on-one with each business that inquires about our services. For us, it’s about really getting to know you and your business and all the obstacles and goals that come with it. That way, we can make educated decisions when creating your marketing plan.

How much does it cost to work with you?

Since we don’t have packages, each plan has a custom price range based on a variety of factors.

How do you track results?

One of our main tools is Google Analytics. We also have several tools that allow us to track everything from impressions to conversions to website visits to actual purchases. Finally, we talk with you once per week and sit down with you once per month to review all the information, then make decisions based on data.

Meradise understand the do’s & don’ts of digital marketing and will be able to create impactful and long-lasting impressions on your customers.
